Calculation Error, No Errata[edit]

Glyph of Warding (Blast) CR 6; spell; spell trigger; no reset; spell effect (glyph of warding [blast], 16th-level cleric, 8d8 sonic, DC 14 Reflex Save half damage); multiple targets (all targets within 5 ft.); Search DC 28; Disable Device DC 28. Cost: 680 gp to hire NPC spellcaster.

Glyph of Warding (Blast) is limited to 5d8 sonic damage. For 8d8 sonic damage, Greater Glyph of Warding should be cast, resulting in the following statistics:

Glyph of Warding, Greater (Blast) CR 6; spell; spell trigger; no reset; spell effect (glyph of warding,greater [blast], 16th-level cleric, 8d8 sonic, DC 17 Reflex Save half damage); multiple targets (all targets within 5 ft.); Search DC 31; Disable Device DC 31. Cost: 1,360 gp to hire NPC spellcaster.

The CR 6 is based on average damage.
