Craft Psicrown [Item Creation]

You can create psicrowns, which have multiple psionic effects.

Prerequisite

Manifester level 12th.

Benefit

You can create any psicrown whose prerequisites you meet. Crafting a psicrown takes one day for each 1,000 gp in its base price. To craft a psicrown, you must spend 1/25 of its base price in XP and use up raw materials costing one-half of its base price. Some psicrowns incur extra costs in XP as noted in their descriptions.
