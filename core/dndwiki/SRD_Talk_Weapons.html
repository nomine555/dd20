Proficiencies[edit]

I've been searching a long time, but I can't find the penalties for non weapon proficiency. I feel like links to the ways in which weapons are used should be included here. But maybe I'm wrong.. --Lord.Sorasen 13:35, 23 November 2010 (SDT)

range[edit]

does the range change for weapon size? Does a huge bow shoot farther than a tiny bow? —The preceding unsigned comment was added by 173.245.50.116 (talk • contribs) . Please sign your posts!

That's a good question. I don't think I've ever seen a rule for changing ranges. I don't think the game gets too unbalanced as is, though. If you were to house rule it, I don't think I would make it change by more than 25% per size category, otherwise you are just asking for your players to find loopholes. 17:22, 3 March 2012 (MST)
