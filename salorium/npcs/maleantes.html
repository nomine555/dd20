RUFIANS (3)

Glum, Brilos and Folen are three criminals that belong to the band of 'El Tuerto'. They are second-rate thieves who survive by threatening and extorting prostitutes in the docks area. They are cowards by nature and their victims are always weak people. Taking advantage of the beasts horde attack, these three criminals have seen the opportunity to act without any restriction.
