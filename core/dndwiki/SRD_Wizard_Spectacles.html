Wizard's Spectacles
Price: 2,800 gp
Body Slot: Eyes
Caster Level: 3rd
Aura: Faint; (DC 18) Divination
Activation: Standard and —
Weight: —

These gold rimmed crystal glasses have runes inscribed upon them.

These glasses are worn usually by more wealthy wizards and magic users. These glasses allow you to cast Read Magic and Detect Magic at will, and 2/Day you can cast Identify. Also, these glasses give a +2 Bonus to Spellcraft and Knowledge (Arcana) checks.

  • Prereqs: Craft Wonderous Item, Detect Magic, Read Magic, Identify
  • Cost to Create: 1,400gp, 112 XP, 3 Days
