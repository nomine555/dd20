Energy Substitution [Metamagic]

Prerequisites

Any other metamagic feat, Knowledge (arcana) 5 ranks.

Benefit

You choose one type of energy: acid, cold, electricity, fire, or sonic. When employing a spell with the acid, cold, electricity, fire, or sonic designator, you can modify the spell to use your chosen type of energy instead. The altered spell uses a spell slot of the spellâ€™s normal level.

The altered spell works normally in all respects except the type of damage dealt.

Special

You can gain this feat multiple times. Each time the feat applies to a different type of energy.
