Chaos Diamond: This lustrous gemstone is uncut and about the size of a human fist. The gem grants its possessor the following powers:

  • Confusion, lesser
  • Magic circle against law
  • Word of chaos
  • Cloak of chaos

Each power is usable 1d4 times per day. This is rolled for each power individually and the character does not know the result.
