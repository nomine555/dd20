Rage
Enchantment (Compulsion) [Mind-Affecting]
Level: Brd 2, Madness 3, Sor/Wiz 3
Components: V, S
Casting time: 1 standard action
Range: Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
Targets: One willing living creature per three levels, no two of which may be more than 30 ft. apart
Duration: Concentration + 1 round/level (D)
Saving Throw: None
Spell Resistance: Yes


Each affected creature gains a +2 morale bonus to Strength and Constitution, and a +1 morale bonus on Will saves, and a –2 penalty to AC. The effect is otherwise identical with a barbarian’s rage except that the subjects aren’t fatigued at the end of the rage.
