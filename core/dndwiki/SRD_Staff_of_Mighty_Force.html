Mighty Force

This staff has three powers.

  • Shield (quickened, 1 charge, may be activated once per round)
  • Forcecage (1 charge)
  • Crushing hand (1 charge)

Caster Level: 21st; Prerequisites: Craft Staff, Craft Epic Staff, Quicken Spell, crushing hand, orcecage, shield; Market Price: 265,000 gp.
