Feats compendium[edit]

Would such a thing infringes copyright? If not, I think it would be worth doing it. We already have the NBoF, a compendium of WotC’s would be a nice addition and would help establish dandwiki as a reference.
David Latapie (✒ | @) — blog 04:58, 11 April 2007 (MDT)

WotC already has a compedium of their own feats. See the SRD main page. It should also now show up on the Feats page.--Dmilewski 13:43, 11 April 2007 (MDT)
here is the link to the WotC Feat Index if you are curious. --Green Dragon 20:46, 11 April 2007 (MDT)
Yes, I know it and use it (although I prefer better-layouted, officious versions). But to have such a list here would help making us a one-stop reference point and would also allow us to integrate NBoF and homebrew feats. Plus, the discussion page could be used for disambiguation. Yeah, I know is it a long work, but maybe that can be built over time.
David Latapie (✒ | @) — blog 04:57, 12 April 2007 (MDT)
If you would like to compile such I thing D&D Wiki would be more than happy to have it here, however.... I know I am not interested in adding it, and do not have the time to do such a thing. --Green Dragon 21:04, 12 April 2007 (MDT)


Crystalkeep was making pdf's to show indexes of feats, monsters, classes etc etc. Actualy works great. Though I doubt wizards approves. It makes for easy and quick power building. Without knowing all of the books out of your head... I do have to note: you are way better of if you actualy have the books! http://www.crystalkeep.com/d20/index.php enjoy--Damrus 08:06, 19 December 2009 (MST)

Uh...[edit]

Why is fast healing part of this table? Is that the name of an epic feat or is it misplaced? –EldritchNumen 17:55, 6 January 2008 (MST)

It was misplaced. Thanks. --Dmilewski 18:08, 6 January 2008 (MST)
Actually, there is an epic feat called Fast Healing, which grants the benefits of the special ability of the same name. —Sledged (talk) 19:30, 6 January 2008 (MST)

Buggy Link[edit]

Mounted Combat[edit]

The mounted combat link sends you off to the general mounted combat information page, rather than the actual feat description. This problem is also found on the Ride skill page.--Finfreeze 20:54, 14 March 2008 (MDT)

Got it. Thanks. —Sledged (talk) 21:09, 14 March 2008 (MDT)

Two Weapons Fighting[edit]

The link points to the "Two Weapons Fighting" article instead of the "Two-Weapon Fighting (Feat)" article. -- 82.233.252.130 13:11, 17 December 2008 (MST)

Nice catch and thanks. For an admin with the ability, the proper link should go to SRD:Two-Weapon Fighting (Feat).   Hooper   talk    contribs    email   13:32, 17 December 2008 (MST)
That is a GREAT catch! Thats been there for over half a year and no one has realised! (Also, I fixed it.) --Sabre070 16:44, 17 December 2008 (MST)
There are a lot of buggy links in the SRD that have been there for over a year ;). I just change them whenever I see them. --Aarnott 22:19, 17 December 2008 (MST)
Buggy links came from my automated linking. I must have added 20,000 links, so there are bound to be some outliers.--Dmilewski 20:00, 18 December 2008 (MST)


Adding New Feats[edit]

So lets say somebody has a lot of free time and wants to add a lot of feats to this page how would you go about doing that is there a particular reason that we have such a limited supply of feats and why so many of them are psionic feats or will I have to find another way to appease my feat fetish? Complicated Stares 21:43 December 18 2009 (MST)

99% of stuff wizardds created in't under OGL. we can't post it. --Name Violation 21:53, 18 December 2009 (MST)
DnD Feats (3.5e) or 4e Feats for your items/OGC items (from the book) of course. --Green Dragon 23:39, 18 December 2009 (MST)
