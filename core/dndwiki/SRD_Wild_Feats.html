Wild Feats

The feats in this category share the characteristic of relating to the ability to use wild shape as a druid. These feats require the character to have the ability to use wild shape before acquiring the feat.

  • Colossal Wild Shape
  • Diminutive Wild Shape
  • Dragon Wild Shape
  • Fine Wild Shape
  • Gargantuan Wild Shape
  • Improved Elemental Wild Shape
  • Magical Beast Companion
  • Magical Beast Wild Shape
  • Plant Wild Shape
  • Vermin Wild Shape
