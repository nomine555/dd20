Incite Rage [Epic]

Prerequisites

Cha 25, greater rage class feature.

Benefit

When the character enters a rage, he or she can incite a barbarian rage in any or all allies within 60 feet. (Any ally who doesn’t wish to become enraged is unaffected.) The ally gains +4 to Strength, +4 to Consitution, and a +2 morale bonus on Will saves, but takes a –2 penalty to AC, for as long as the character remains raging. The rage of affected allies lasts a number of rounds equal to 3 + their Consitution modifier, regardless of whether they remain within 60 feet of the character. This is otherwise identical with normal barbarian rage (including the fatigue at its end).

Special

This is a mind-affecting effect.
