Academic Tome

These manuscripts are the highly sought-after works of scholars who have scripted their experiences, knowledge of and opinion on various subjects. Some tell of the relationships between the planes of existence. Others detail a few basic rules of engineering and architecture. A character must study an academic tome for a total of 48 hours for a minimum of 6 days to gain a +2 informed bonus any Intelligence skill related on the particular topic the book discusses, be it History, Arcane, Religion, etc. The bonus is permanent and stacks with others.
