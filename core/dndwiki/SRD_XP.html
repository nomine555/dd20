Experience

This Adventure is designed for 1-3 level characters so the Experience Chart is quite simple. Of course you can use the Challenge Rating of the encounters, and adjust for your own level of advancement using a multiplier.

Table: Experience Points Awards
Encounter Level XP per Encounter
per group of players
1 300 XP
2 600 XP
3 900 XP
4 1,350 XP
5 1,800 XP

Using this chart, the characters should advance from the 1st level to 2nd level nearly at the end of this adventure.
