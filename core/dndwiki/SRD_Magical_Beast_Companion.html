Magical Beast Companion [Wild][Epic]

Prerequisites

Knowledge (nature) 24 ranks, wild shape 6/day.

Benefit

The following magical beasts are added to the lists of animal companions from which you can select.

1st Level (no adjustment)
  • Stirge
  • Darkmantle
4th Level (-3)
  • Hippogriff
  • Shocker lizard
7th Level (-6)
  • Cockatrice
  • Ankheg
  • Griffon
  • Owlbear
  • Sea cat*
10th Level (-9)
  • Basilisk
  • Digerster
  • Girallon
  • Spider eater
13th Level (-12)
  • Bulette
  • Chimera
  • Remorhaz
16th Level (-15)
  • Gorgon
  • Gray render
Special

Creatures marked with an asterisk are available only in an aquatic environment.
