SMELTER BEAST

Those beings are covered by hard metallic skin from head to toe. His appendages ending in hard hooves fire sparks when hitting hard ground. A pair of twisted horns jut down from his skull almost reaching it’s mouth, wicked sharp spines protrude from it’s back. 

Size/Type: Medium Demon
Hit Dice: 2d8+2 (12 hp)
Initiative: +4
Speed: 40 ft (8 squares)
Armor Class: 12 (+2 natural), touch 10, flat-footed 12
Base Attack/Grapple: +0/-2
Attack: Claw +2 melee (1d4+1)
Full Attack: 2 claws +2 melee (2d4+1)
Space/Reach: 5 ft/5 ft
Special Qualities: low-light vision, tremorsense 30 ft., hiveminded
Saves: Fort 4, Ref 5, Will 1
Abilities: Str 12, Dex 10, Con 14, Int 1, Wis 12, Cha 4
Skills: Jump +4, Listen +1, Spot +1, Hide +1
Feats: Improved Initiative
Challenge Rating: 1
Alignment: Always neutral


COMBAT

Melting beasts hide by burrowing a few feet into the ground, then ambush any creatures they sense walking nearby. They work in groups to bring down their foes and attack by using their scythed appendages.

Tunneler (Ex):  A melting beast can burrow through solid rock at a speed of 5 feet. It does not leave a usable tunnel unless it chooses to.

Hiveminded (Ex): Melting beasts are in constant communication with every other melting beast within 30 feet. No melting beast in a group is considered flanked or flatfooted unless all of them are. All Melting beast within 30 feet of at least one other melting beast in the same group act at the same initiative (make a single initiative roll for the entire group).

A melting beast receives a +1 morale bonus on Will saves for every 3 other Melting beast within 30 feet. However, if a melting beast fails a Will save against anything that causes a shaken, frightened, or panicked effect, all other Melting beast in the group also become shaken, frightened, or panicked, respectively.

Low-Light Vision (Ex): A melting beast can see twice as far as a human in starlight, moonlight, torchlight, and similar conditions of shadowy illumination.

Tremorsense (30 ft) (Ex): Melting beasts are highly sensitive to ground vibrations, allowing them to pinpoint the location of any creature within 30 feet that is in contact with the ground, unless that creature is standing perfectly still.
