Grip of Iron
Psychometabolism
Level: Psychic warrior 1
Display: Visual
Manifesting Time: 1 immediate action
Range: Personal
Target: You
Duration: 1 round/level
Power Points: 1

You can manifest this power with an instant thought, quickly enough to gain the benefit of the power in the current round.
