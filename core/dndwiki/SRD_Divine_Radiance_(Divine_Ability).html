Divine Radiance

Prerequisite: Good alignment, Sun domain.

Benefit: The deity can shed light in a (10 feet x divine rank) emanation from its body. This light counters and dispels all darkness effects (unless created by a deity of higher rank) and causes pain and damage to undead creatures, who take 2d8 points of damage per round with no save.
