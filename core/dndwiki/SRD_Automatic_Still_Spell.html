Automatic Still Spell [Epic]

Prerequisites

Still Spell, Spellcraft 27 ranks, ability to cast 9th-level arcane or divine spells.

Benefit

The character may cast all 0-, 1st-, 2nd-, and 3rd-level spells as stilled spells without using higher-level spell slots.

Special

A character can gain this feat multiple times. Each time a character takes the feat, the spells of his or her next three lowest spell levels can now be stilled with no adjustment to their spell slots.
