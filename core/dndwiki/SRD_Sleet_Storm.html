Sleet Storm
Conjuration (Creation) [Cold]
Level: Drd 3, Sor/Wiz 3, Weather 4
Components: V, S, M/DF
Casting time: 1 standard action
Range: Long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
Area: Cylinder (40-ft. radius, 20 ft. high)
Duration: 1 round/level
Saving Throw: None
Spell Resistance: No
