Extra Sense Enhancement

Prerequisites: Wis 29.

Benefit: The deity chooses one sense to enhance. The ability can extend a senseâ€™s range one mile per divine rank, or it can allow the sense to ignore one type of physical or magical blockage. The enhanced sense can penetrate 10 feet of a physical barrier per rank or 100 feet of a less tangible barrier (such as fog or smoke) per rank.

Notes: Some deities have specific applications of this ability that go beyond the normal senses.

A deity can have this ability multiple times, choosing a different sense each time.

Suggested Portfolio Elements: Any.
